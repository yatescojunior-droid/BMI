Agriculture Department

Introduction

The Agriculture Departmentempowers students with the knowledge and skills needed to improve food production and promote sustainable farming. Areas of study include crop cultivation, livestock management, soil science, and modern agricultural techniques. Students graduate prepared to become innovative farmers, agricultural entrepreneurs, or pursue careers in agribusiness.

What Students Learn

  • Crop production and management (vegetables, grains, fruits)
  • Animal husbandry and livestock care
  • Soil science and sustainable farming methods
  • Agricultural tools, machinery, and technology
  • Agribusiness and farm management
