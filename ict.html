About Computer Information Technology Department

The ICT (Information and Communication Technology) Department prepares students for the digital age. Students learn computer fundamentals, office productivity software, web design, and basic programming. The program also emphasizes problem-solving and digital communication. Graduates are capable of working in IT support, administration, or using technology to enhance other professional fields.
