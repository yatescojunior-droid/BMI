Domestic Science Department

Introduction

The Domestic Science Department focuses on building practical skills for everyday life and professional service industries. Students are trained in catering, nutrition, home management, tailoring, and hospitality. With these skills, graduates are prepared to thrive in the food and fashion industries, provide community services, or launch their own enterprises.

What Students Learn

  • Nutrition and meal planning
  • Food preparation and catering services
  • Sewing, tailoring, and fabric care
  • Home management and budgeting
  • Hospitality and customer service skills
