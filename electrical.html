Electrical Department

Introduction

The Electrical Department provides students with hands-on training in electrical installation, wiring, maintenance, and repair. Students are taught the principles of electricity, safety standards, and modern electrical technologies. By the end of their studies, graduates are well-equipped to work as electricians, technicians, or start their own businesses in the electrical field.

What Students Learn

  • Electrical wiring and installations
  • Maintenance and safety practices
  • Basic electronics and circuits
  • Entrepreneurship in the electrical field
